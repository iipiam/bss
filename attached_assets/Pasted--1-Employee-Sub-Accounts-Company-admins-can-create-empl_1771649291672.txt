
1.Employee Sub-Accounts
Company admins can create employee sub-accounts with their own username and password
Each sub-account has configurable permissions controlling access to: Dashboard, Projects, Financial, Invoices, Services, Clients, and Contractors
Employees log in with their own credentials and only see what they have permission to access
2. Dashboard
Central landing page after login
Displays key metrics and overview of the company's activity
Interface Preference selector lets the user choose between Desktop, iPhone, and iPad layouts to optimize the viewing experience
Quick access to all major sections of the app
3. Project Management
Project Creation and Editing
Create projects with details like name, description, client, status, dates, and budget
Edit and update project information at any time
CPM (Critical Path Method) Scheduling
Server-side implementation of the Critical Path Method algorithm
Calculates the critical path through project tasks
Identifies which tasks are critical (cannot be delayed without delaying the project)
Helps project managers understand scheduling dependencies and priorities
Project-Specific Services
Add services to each project from the company's service catalog
Each service has a configurable pricing method:
Per piece — price per individual unit
Length — price per unit of length
Area — price per unit of area
Hour — price per hour of work
Lump sum — a single fixed price for the entire service
Project-Specific Bills
Track bills associated with each project
Record bill details, amounts, dates, and status
Project-Specific Procurements
Track procurement purchases for each project
Record what was purchased, from whom, costs, and dates
Payment Schedules
Automatic generation of payment schedules for projects
Breaks the project cost into scheduled payment installments
Tracks payment status for each installment
Project Dossier PDF
A comprehensive PDF document containing all project information in one file
Includes financials, tasks, materials, schedules, and all related data
Can be downloaded for offline review or sharing
4. Quotation System
Creating Quotations
Each project can have a quotation
Add services to the quotation with editable pricing methods and amounts
Preview the quotation before finalizing
Quotation Workflow
Quotations can be approved or declined
When declined, a reason is tracked so the team knows why
Quotation PDF
Generate and download a professional PDF of the quotation
Includes company information in the header
Supports both Arabic and English with proper text rendering
Uses Amiri font for Arabic text with connected letters
RTL layout for Arabic content

Subscription and Refund Invoices
Generate invoices specifically for subscription payments

Step 3: Navigating the App
The sidebar on the left shows all sections of the app
Click on any section name to go to that area
On mobile, the sidebar collapses — tap the menu icon at the top to open it
At the top of the page, you will find:
Language toggle — switch between Arabic and English
Theme toggle — switch between light and dark mode
On the Dashboard, you can also choose your Interface Preference — Desktop, iPhone, or iPad layout
Step 4: Managing Clients
Click "Clients" in the sidebar
To add a new client, click the "Add Client" button
Fill in the client's name, contact details, and any other information
Click Save
Your client now appears in the list
To edit a client, click on their record and make changes
To delete a client, click the delete option on their record
Step 5: Managing Contractors
Click "Contractors" in the sidebar
To add a new contractor, click the "Add Contractor" button
Fill in the contractor's details
Click Save
To edit or delete, follow the same steps as with clients
Step 6: Setting Up Your Service Catalog
Click "Services" in the sidebar
Click "Add Service" to create a new service your company offers
Enter the service name, description, and choose a default pricing method:
Per piece — charged per unit
Length — charged by length measurement
Area — charged by area measurement
Hour — charged per hour
Lump sum — one fixed price
Click Save
These services will be available when creating quotations and project service lists
Step 7: Creating and Managing Projects
Creating a Project
Click "Projects" in the sidebar
Click "Add Project"
Fill in the project name, description, select a client, set dates, budget, and status
Click Save
Adding Services to a Project
Open a project by clicking on it
Go to the Services section within the project
Click "Add Service"
Select a service from your catalog
Choose the pricing method and enter the price/quantity
Click Save
Tracking Bills for a Project
Inside a project, go to the Bills section
Click "Add Bill"
Enter the bill details — description, amount, date, status
Click Save
Tracking Procurements for a Project
Inside a project, go to the Procurements section
Click "Add Procurement"
Enter what was purchased, vendor, cost, and date
Click Save
Payment Schedules
Inside a project, go to the Payment Schedule section
The system can automatically generate a payment schedule based on the project total
Each installment shows the amount and due date
Track which payments have been made
CPM Scheduling
Inside a project, go to the Tasks/Schedule section
Add tasks with their durations and dependencies
The system calculates the Critical Path — the longest sequence of tasks that determines the minimum project duration
Tasks on the critical path are highlighted so you know which ones cannot be delayed
Step 8: Creating Quotations
Open a project
Go to the Quotation section
Click "Create Quotation"
Add services to the quotation — you can adjust pricing for each service
Click "Preview" to see how the quotation looks
If satisfied, you can:
Download as PDF — the PDF will include your company info in the header, in either Arabic or English
Approve the quotation to move forward
Decline the quotation with a reason
Step 9: Creating Invoices
Navigate to the Invoices section from the sidebar
Click "Create Invoice"
Fill in the invoice details — client, project, services, amounts
All amounts are displayed in Saudi Riyal (SAR)
Click "Download PDF" to generate a professional invoice
The PDF supports both Arabic and English with proper text rendering
Step 10: Generating Project Agreements
Open a project
Go to the Agreement section
The agreement is generated based on your agreement template (set up in Company Settings)
Placeholders in the template are automatically replaced with actual project and client data
Your Terms and Conditions are included at the bottom
Click "Download PDF" to get the agreement document
Step 11: Downloading the Project Dossier
Open a project
Look for the "Download Dossier" option
This generates a comprehensive PDF that includes everything about the project:
Project details
Financial summary (bills, procurements, payments)
Tasks and schedule
Materials
All related data in one document

Step 14: Company Settings
Editing Company Information

Update your company name, email, phone, address, and details
Click Save
This information will appear in the headers of all PDFs you generate
Customizing Agreement Templates
In Settings, go to the Agreement Template section
Edit your template text
Use placeholders (like {client_name}, {project_name}) that will be automatically filled in when agreements are generated
You can add custom placeholders for information specific to your business
Click Save
Managing Terms and Conditions
In Settings, find the Terms and Conditions section

Click Save

Step 17: Switching Language

All text switches to the selected language

English uses LTR (left-to-right) layout
PDFs will also generate in the currently selected language
