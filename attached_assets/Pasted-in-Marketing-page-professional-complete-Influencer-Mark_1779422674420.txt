Title: "Influencer Marketing Calculator"

Make it modern, clean, with emojis and good color coding.

### Main Sections / Tabs:

1. **Engagement Rate Calculator**

   - Inputs: Followers, Likes, Comments, Shares, Saves

   - Calculate: Total Engagements, ER% = (Total Engagements / Followers) * 100

   - Color code: Green ≥5%, Yellow 3-4.99%, Red <3%

   - Show rating (Excellent / Good / Average / Low)

2. **Sponsorship Rate Estimator**

   - Input: Number of Followers + Engagement Rate

   - Estimate sponsorship price per post using these formulas:

        - Nano (1K-10K): $10–$100

        - Micro (10K-100K): $100–$500

        - Macro (100K-1M): $500–$5000

   - Use Saudi Market rates (in SAR) as default:

        - Price per Post = (Followers / 1000) * CPM

        - Default CPM for Food niche in KSA: 25–80 SAR

   - Allow user to adjust CPM

3. **Cost & ROI Calculator (For Brands)**

   - Inputs:

        - Sponsorship Cost (SAR)

        - Expected Reach / Impressions

        - Expected Engagements

        - Average Order Value (AOV)

        - Conversion Rate (%)

   - Calculations:

        - CPE = Cost / Total Engagements

        - CPM = (Cost / Impressions) * 1000

        - Estimated Sales = Engagements * Conversion Rate

        - Estimated Revenue = Estimated Sales * AOV

        - ROI % = ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) * 100

        - ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

4. **Influencer Earnings Dashboard**

   - Estimate monthly earnings if they do:

        - 4 posts per month

        - 8 stories per month

        - Mix of sponsored + affiliate

5. **Comparison Table**

   - Allow adding multiple influencers

   - Columns: Name, Followers, ER%, Est. Post Price (SAR), CPE, ROI%, Rating

   - Show average values at the bottom

   - Download as CSV button
