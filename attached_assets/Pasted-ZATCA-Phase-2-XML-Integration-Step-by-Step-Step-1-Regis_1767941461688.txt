ZATCA Phase 2 XML Integration - Step by Step
Step 1: Register Your System with ZATCA
•	Go to ZATCA's Fatoora Portal (online)
•	Register your POS/ERP system as an "EGS" (E-Invoice Generation Solution)
•	Get an OTP (one-time password) for authentication
________________________________________
Step 2: Generate Security Keys
•	Create a pair of encryption keys (public + private)
•	Private key = stays secret on your server
•	Public key = shared with ZATCA
•	Generate a CSR (Certificate Signing Request)
________________________________________
Step 3: Get Your Certificate (CSID)
•	Submit CSR to ZATCA
•	First you get a "Compliance CSID" for testing
•	After passing tests, you get a "Production CSID" for real invoices
•	This certificate proves your invoices are authentic
________________________________________
Step 4: Build the XML Invoice
When a sale happens, your system must create an XML file with:
•	Header: Invoice number, date, type, UUID
•	Seller info: Your business name, VAT number, address
•	Buyer info: Customer details (for B2B invoices)
•	Line items: Each product/service with price, quantity, VAT
•	Totals: Subtotal, VAT amount, grand total
•	Previous Invoice Hash: Links to your last invoice (creates a chain)
________________________________________
Step 5: Sign the Invoice
•	Calculate a "hash" (unique fingerprint) of the invoice
•	Sign this hash with your private key
•	Add the digital signature to the XML
•	Generate the 9-tag QR code
________________________________________
Step 6: Send to ZATCA
For B2B invoices (Standard):
1.	Send XML to ZATCA API
2.	Wait for approval (real-time)
3.	ZATCA adds their stamp
4.	You get back the approved invoice
5.	Then give to customer
For B2C invoices (Simplified):
1.	Generate invoice locally with signature
2.	Give to customer immediately
3.	Send copy to ZATCA within 24 hours
________________________________________
Step 7: Store Everything
•	Keep all invoices for 6 years
•	Cannot delete or modify them
•	Must be digitally accessible
________________________________________
What You Need to Build
Component	Purpose
Key Manager	Store encryption keys securely
XML Generator	Convert invoice data to UBL 2.1 XML
Hash Calculator	SHA-256 hash of each invoice
Digital Signer	Sign invoices with ECDSA
QR Generator	Create 9-tag TLV QR codes
ZATCA API Client	Send/receive from ZATCA servers
Invoice Chain	Track previous invoice hashes
